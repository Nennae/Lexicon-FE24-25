GOING TO BOSTON - 3 DICE GAME

* function to throw the dices

* variable to store max score: 18

* variable to store the end score of the players

* an empty array to push and store each highest numbered dice

* a loop to iterate over the three dices

* a conditional check, nested inside the above loop to exclude the highest numbered dice of each throw

* when all dices are thrown - iterate over the now populated array and sum the numbers

* assign above sum to the end score variable 

* a conditional check to compare the players end score to see who wins
